Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Get instant updates on your tank's water levels through our mobile app.

Smart Alerts

Receive notifications when water levels are too high or too low to avoid overflow or shortages.

Easy Installation

Quick and straightforward setup that integrates with your existing water tanks.

How It Works

  1. Install the Sensor: Easily attach the IoT sensor to your water tank.

  2. Connect to the App: Sync the sensor with the Wakra app for real-time monitoring.

  3. Receive Alerts: Get notified when the water reaches set thresholds.

  4. Monitor and Control: View water usage trends and take action when needed.

Benefits

  • Save Water: Avoid water wastage by staying informed about tank levels.
  • Peace of Mind: No more worrying about overflow or running out of water.
  • Cost-Effective: Efficient water management leads to lower utility bills.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Help conserve water, a precious resource.
